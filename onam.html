ONAM: THE HARVEST FESTIVAL OF KERALA

Onam is an annual harvest festival and cultural celebration in Kerala, India, celebrated in August-September to mark the homecoming of King Mahabali and thank the gods for the year's harvest. Key traditions include creating elaborate Pookalams (flower carpets), enjoying a grand vegetarian feast known as Onasadya, watching Vallam Kali (boat races), and participating in various traditional games and dances like Pulikali (tiger dance) and Thiruvathira Kali
